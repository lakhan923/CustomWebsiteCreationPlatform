About me

Hello! I'm Laiba Khan, a 25-year-old web developer with a passion for creating beautiful and functional websites. I am currently studying Information and Communication Technology at Gradia Jyväskylä, Finland, with my course expected to end on August 8, 2025. My background includes a diverse range of technical skills and experiences that have shaped my approach to web development.

My journey in technology began with a preparatory TUVA course at Gradia Jyväskylä, where I completed 38 weeks of foundational education. I furthered my knowledge with the INTEGRA course at the University of Jyväskylä, focusing on programming, web design, and digital competence. Additionally, I undertook Finnish language courses to enhance my communication skills and cultural understanding.

I am a hardworking, self-starting, reliable, and well-organized individual with a keen interest in continuous learning. I thrive both in group settings and independently, and I am driven by the desire to use my technical skills to benefit organizations and help others. My goal is to develop websites that not only look great but also offer an exceptional user experience.

Here are some of my technical skills:

  • Visual Studio and Visual studio code
  • Python Basics
  • C# Basics
  • Linux
  • Git and GitHub
  • Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Excel etc
  • Notepad++, Sublime Text
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, jQuery
  • Databases
  • WordPress
  • SEO "Search Engine Optimization"
  • Graphical User Interfaces (Windows Form App)

In terms of website development services, my fees are designed to be flexible and accommodating to different needs and budgets. I offer competitive pricing based on the complexity and scope of the project. Whether you need a basic business website or a fully-featured e-commerce platform, I am committed to delivering high-quality work that meets your specific needs and budget. Please feel free to contact me for a personalized quote and to discuss how I can help bring your project to life.

I take pride in my attention to detail and my commitment to delivering high-quality results for every project I undertake. I'm excited to continue expanding my skills and contributing to projects that make a difference.
